This application uses sound materials from the audiovisual installation “GrainStick” designed by Pierre Jodlowski at IRCAM.

Hold your device horizontally and tilt it up and down to let the sound particles flow (watch the circle sliding back and forth). Tilt carefully to explore delicate sounds.

This application is designed for mobile devices under iOS or Android. It won't work properly on this device.
