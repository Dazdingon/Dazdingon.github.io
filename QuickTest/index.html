CoSiMa Sound Checks

Drone Birds Monks Rainstick WWRY:R

This is a collection of prototype applications that have been developped in the framework of the CoSiMa project.

The applications are based on existing and upcoming HTML5 web standards auch as the Web Audio and Device Motion APIs. They should work on recent iOS and Android mobile devices using up-to-date browsers such as Chrome and Safari.

The CoSiMa Sound Checks have been developed at IRCAM's Sound Music Motion Interaction group. Some of their components have been developed in the framework of the WAVE project.

The projects CoSiMa and WAVE are supported by the French National Research Agency ANR.

This application is designed for mobile devices under iOS or Android. It won't work properly on this device.
